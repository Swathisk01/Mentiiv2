Recommended Books on Mental Health

The Power of Positive Thinking

Author: Norman Vincent Peale

Description: A transformative book that holds the key to unlocking our inner potential.

THE MAGIC OF THINKING BIG

Author: David J. Schwartz

Description: The Magic Of Thinking Big tells us that studying our setbacks will help pave our way to success. The difference between success and failure is found in one's attitudes towards setbacks and other disappointing situations.

Man's search for meaning

Author: Viktor Frankl

Description: The core of Frankl's philosophy is that a man's deepest desire is to find meaning in his life, and if he can find that meaning, he can survive anything. Frankl found meaning in his experiences in the concentration camp by deciding that he was going to use his suffering as an opportunity to make himself a better person.
